The Roady

Obilisly i need to change the vehicle from South Australian plaits to Victoria plates so i had to get a road worthy certificate so i did and it failed the first enpection here is the list of thing needing to be fixed to get this vehicle good to go for the swap

Road worthy Cirtificate

These were what needed to be fixed to get aprovle


  1. Brakes: Below wear indicator,Driver rear pads

  2. Lamps,Signals,Reflectors ect: Requiers adjustment,Driver and passenger Dipped beam (low beam)

  3. Lamps,Signals,Reflectors ect: Requires adjustment,Driving lights requires adjustment

  4. Lamps,Signals,Reflectors ect:Non compliant,Additional lighting,Lightbar mounted above bull bar,causing driver vision impairment and protrusion/sharp egde hazard

  5. Lamps,Signals,Reflectors ect:Not funtioning,Additional lighting,bull bar mounted park lights not working,lenses mounted upside down park light should be on outside

  6. Lamps,Signals,Reflectors ect:Not funtioning,Warning lamps: Check engine (mil) not illuminating with ignition on

  7. Seats and seat belts: damaged,2nd row middle seat belt

  8. Seats and seat belts: missing 3rd row head restraints

  9. Body and chassis: Worn hinges,Drivers door,Upper hinge

  10. Other items: incorrectly fitted ,Electorical wiring, driving light switch not fitted correctly

  11. Modification: Non compliant CB Antenna bracket fitted facing forward,protrusion/sharp edge hazzard

  12. Modification: Return to manufacturer specification,Body,Runing boards missingend caps,protrusion/sharp edge hazzard
